Start/Stop (AGM & EFB) Solutions

Power designed for modern fuel efficiency.

Vehicles with Start/Stop systems cannot use standard batteries. We stock the specialized Raylite **AGM (Absorbed Glass Mat)** and **EFB (Enhanced Flooded Battery)** ranges. These batteries are built to handle the constant cycling and deep discharge required by this technology, ensuring your system runs efficiently without failure.

  • AGM for higher performance and maximum cycling life.
  • EFB as a cost-effective, durable solution for standard Start/Stop cars.
  • Expert fitment required to maintain system integrity.
