The Service Difference: Our Commitment to Centurion

The First Battery Centre Centurion team, based right here in Hennopspark, offers a complete suite of services backed by national trust and decades of expertise. We're here to solve your power problems professionally and efficiently.

1. Free, Comprehensive Battery & Charging System Diagnostics

The foundation of our service is trust, and we earn it by providing expert analysis before we ever recommend a new purchase. Don't guess about your battery's health; let our fully trained technicians find the answer.

  • Free Battery Check: We perform a complete analysis of your existing battery's State of Health (SOH) and Cold Cranking Amps (CCA) using professional diagnostic tools.
  • Alternator Check: A dead battery is often a symptom, not the problem. We check your entire vehicle charging system and alternator output to ensure the new battery will perform correctly, preventing immediate failure.
  • Starter Test: We verify that your starter motor is drawing the correct current, ensuring reliable starts and pinpointing the true source of any starting difficulty.

2. Professional Fitment and Supply (Raylite Specialists)

We don't just sell batteries; we ensure you get the *exact* right one and that it’s installed precisely—critical for modern vehicles.

  • Expert Battery Supply: We stock the region’s largest range of high-quality, Raylite OEM-approved batteries for every application: Passenger, Commercial, and Leisure.
  • Precise, Professional Fitment: Our team uses specialized equipment to install the new unit precisely, ensuring the connection is secure and the vehicle's memory systems (electronics, radio) are maintained during the changeover. We guarantee the fitment is professional.

3. Comprehensive Support and Environmental Responsibility

Our commitment to Centurion extends beyond the sale. We focus on long-term vehicle health and environmental stewardship.

  • Battery Charging: We offer charging services for batteries that have simply run flat due to an electrical drain, often saving you the cost of a replacement.
